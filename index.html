    About the numbers

    How the figures are calculated

    Vote shares are calculated from valid votes cast. Turnout uses valid plus invalid ballots as a share of the electorate. “Other” combines candidates outside the named party columns in the source data. Seat gains use the result classification supplied in the dataset. Winning margins are classified as extremely marginal below 2%, marginal from 2% to under 10%, semi-safe from 10% to under 20%, majority from 20% to 40%, and extreme majority above 40%.
